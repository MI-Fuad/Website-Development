How to get here

Directions from Goldsmiths:

  • Head north-east towards Lewisham Way/A20
  • Turn left onto Lewisham Way/A2/A20
  • Turn right onto A20
  • Turn right onto New Cross Rd/A2
  • Turn right onto Charlton Rd/B210
  • Turn right onto Torrance Cl, and you have reached your destination.
